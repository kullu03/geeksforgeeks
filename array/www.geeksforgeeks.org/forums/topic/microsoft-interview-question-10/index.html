    Let the coordinates of three corners be (x1, y1), (x2, y2) and (x3, y3). And the given point be (x, y)

    1) Calculate the area of the given triangle using the following standard formula.

    Area A = [x1(x2-x3) + x2(x3 – x1) + x3 (x1-x2)]/2

    2) Calculate the area of the triangle formed by (x, y), (x2, y2) and (x3, y3). Let this area be A1.

    2) Calculate the area of the triangle formed by (x1, y1), (x, y) and (x3, y3). Let this area be A2.

    3) Calculate the area of the triangle formed by (x1, y1), (x2, y2) and (x, y). Let this area be A3.

    4) IF (x, y) lies inside the triangle, then A1 + A2 + A3 must be equal to A.
