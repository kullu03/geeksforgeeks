    In a cricket match 2 batsmen were playing. both of them where on 94

    last 3 balls were left and 7 runs needed to win. How both of them can attain century without getting out?

    The Above Solution is not correct.The only possible solution is

    1.First batsman Hits a Six.(Completes his Century)

    Now balls remaining 2 and runs required 1

    2.First batsman wanted to take a single run.Both batsman ran but the Umpire found that the second player was way out of his crease when the baller was balling the ball (which the baller didn’t notice .Of course lots of pressure on bowler).Thus Umpire do not count this as a valid run..But the strike is changed .And Thus second batsman would now face the ball.

    Now balls remaing 1 runs required 1 and II’nd player on strike.

    3.Second batsman Hits a six and completes his century.
