    Initially, only one person knows the secret. In five minutes, he can share the secret to 2 others. So, in total 3 people know the secret at the end of five minutes. In the next five minutes, these 3 persons, can share the secret to 2 persons each. So, at the end of 10 minutes, 9 persons know the secret (3 persons initially, each shared the secret to 2 others 2*3)

    So, the sequence continues as 1, 3, 9, 27, 81, 243, 729, 2187. In total, 35 minutes to share it to 768 persons in total.
