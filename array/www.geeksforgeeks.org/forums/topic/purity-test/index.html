    suppose, spoon can contains 100mL of tea/coffee.

    1 step: – 1 spoon tea to coffee bottle.

    means, 900 mL pure coffee, 1100 mL mix coffee(with 100 mL tea i.e. 1:10 ratio)

    2 step : – if you take 1 spoon(100mL ) of mixed coffee,

    that will be (100*10/11=99.99ml Coffee) + (100*1/11=0.11 mL Tea)

    after taking spoon-full out Coffee jug contains , (1000-99.ml=900.11mL Cofee+99.99mL Tea)

    So final :- when all transfer completed -

    Tea jug contains, 900.11 mL Tea + 99.99 mL Coffee

    Coffee jug contains ,900.11mL cofee + 99.99ml tea

    that will be true for all NmL spoon
