    Actually the answer lies in the problem itself!!We have to consider two cases here:

    Case 1 : Lets say the guy speaks truth on day 1 itself out of the given three days.so this means that the guy will lie on Day2 and Day3!!!!for this case 1 we came at the following results:

    Day 1: He lies on Monday and Tuesday.(assuming Day 1 to be true and Day 2 and Day 3 to be false)

    Day 2: Day 2 will not be Thursday,Saturday or Sunday so the previous day(Day 1 here) will not be be Wednesday,Friday and Sunday!!

    Day 3: He will speak truth on either Wednesday or Friday!

    But Day 1 should not be Wednesday and Friday.So Assumption in Case 1 goes wrong.We move onto Case 2.

    Case 2: In this case we assume that on Day 1 and Day 2 the guy lies and Day 3 he speaks truth.Now according to our assumptions place the facts on the three days

    Day 1 : The guy speaks truth on either Monday or Tuesday.

    Day 2 : Day 2 will not be Thursday,Saturday or Sunday.So the next day(Day 3 here when he actually speaks truth) will not be Friday,Sunday or Monday.

    Day 3: He lies on Wednesday and Friday.(This Day i.e. Day 3 will not be Monday)

    So Tuesday comes out to be the only day when he speaks truth.
