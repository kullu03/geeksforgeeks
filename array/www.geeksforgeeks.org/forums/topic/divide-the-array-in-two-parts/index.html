    This puzzle can be solved by :

    Step 1: Sort the array and put in different container let (say) Arr.

    Step 2:Take highest in one sub-array and 2nd highest in other one and delete from container.

    Step 3: Compare and iterate the sum between two subarrays . The lower sum array will have oppurtunity to take highest element left in the container.

    repeat until container is empty .

    Step 4: Check if two subarrays are same.
