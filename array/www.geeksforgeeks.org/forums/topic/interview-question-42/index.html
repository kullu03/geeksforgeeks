    To number the pages of a book, the printer used 2989 digits. How many pages did the book have?

    a)102 b)515 c)1024 d)2023

    In general,

    A(x) = 4(x – 999) + 2889 where 1000 <= x <= 9999

    The above general equation holds for number of pages from 1000 to 9999. We can come up with similar equations for other ranges.

    Here A(x) = 2989, so x = 1024 pages.

    Pages (No of Pages) x (No. of Digits)

    1 – 9 1 x 9 = 9

    10 – 99 2 x 90 = 180

    100 – 999 3 x 900 = 2700

    till page 1- 999 total 9 + 180 + 2700 = 2889 digits are used.

    now only 2989 – 2889 = 100 are left.

    From page no 999 onward every page no. will have 4 digits each

    so 100 / 4 = 25 more pages will added.

    So total no. of pages = 999 + 25 = 1024
