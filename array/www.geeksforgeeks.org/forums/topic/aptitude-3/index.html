    A man climbing in a rock at 24 km/hr and downhill 36 km/hr. determine avg speed?

    its answer is 28.8 km/hr.The formula to calculate average speed is TOTAL DISTANCE/TOTAL TIME

    so let the distance from bottom to top be x.

    time taken to go up=x/24

    time taken to return back=x/36

    so average speed = total distance/toatl time=2x/((x/24)+(x/36))=28.8

    Avg Velocity = Toatl Distance /Total time

    let’s assume D is distance , V1 ,V2 are velocities.

    Total Distance = D+D=2D

    Total Time = D/V1 + D/V2= D(V1+V2)/V1V2

    Avg Velocity = 2D/ (D(V1+V2)/V1V2)= 2V1V2/(V1+V2)
